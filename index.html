About Al'Ameen Propagation center

Al'Ameen Islamic Propagation Centre (AIPC) was founded to carry out Da'wah, support community education, and provide social services that uplift families and individuals. We focus on respectful outreach, Islamic education, youth empowerment and community service projects across regions.

Our Mission

To provide accurate knowledge of Islam, support converts, run community programs (health, education), and build strong, compassionate communities through Da'wah and service.

  • Islamic education & Qur'anic classes
  • Community outreach & social support
  • Youth engagement & mentorship
